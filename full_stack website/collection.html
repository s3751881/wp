TUESDAY MAKEUP TREND FROM M.A.C COSMESTIC

MONOCHROMATIC PINK CRUSH

" Rosy, stained lips and flush cheeks radiate major Valentine vibes. Get this monochromatic look in three easy steps, featuring Powder Kiss Lipstick in your favourite shade of pink. "
  • STEP 1 : Using a 217S Brush, apply Powder Kiss Lipstick in the pink shade of your choice on eyelids. Concentrate color near lashes and fade out to the creases.
  • STEP 2 : Brush your Powder Kiss Lipstick on apples of cheeks with a 159S Brush, using low, wide strokes for a burnished effect.
  • STEP 3 : Tap your Powder Kiss Lipstick in the centre of lips with a 239S Brush, then feather out the colour to edges.
